### Open Source Projects

**1. An open-source and powerful video subtitle translation assistant: VideoCaptioner.**

Provides a visual operation interface, requires no complex configuration, supports intelligent subtitle segmentation, correction, optimization, translation, and one-click generation of impressive dual-subtitle videos.

GitHub: https://github.com/WEIFENG2333/VideoCaptioner

Features:

- Use powerful speech recognition engine without GPU requirement to generate accurate subtitles;
- LLM-based intelligent segmentation and sentence breaking for more natural and fluid subtitle reading;
- AI subtitle multi-threaded optimization and translation, adjusting subtitle format for more idiomatic and professional expression;
- Supports batch video subtitle synthesis to improve processing efficiency;
- Intuitive subtitle editing and viewing interface with real-time preview and quick editing;
- Low model token consumption and built-in basic LLM model ensuring out-of-the-box usage;
- Supports video download from major domestic and international video platforms such as Bilibili, Youtube, etc.;
- Provides rich subtitle style templates and supports export of multiple subtitle formats such as SRT, ASS, TXT, etc.
